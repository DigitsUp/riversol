Sample Journey — Converting Free Trial to First Purchase

Riversol's sample program is one of the most powerful acquisition tools in the business. Every sample recipient is a warm lead — someone who already has the product in hand. We reviewed the original flow, identified what wasn't working, and rebuilt the journey from the ground up.

The rebuilt 15-day flow is engaging sample customers earlier and more specifically than the previous version — with product-focused emails starting Day 15 and a structured path to conversion by Day 33. Open rates are holding strong across the entire sequence, and the trial completion window (Days 21–29) is where the majority of conversions are happening.
The old flow led with a 30% discount. The new flow leads with product education and value framing — and surfaces a 20% offer slightly earlier (Day 33 vs. Day 35). The new flow also introduces an entry-level singles path starting at $18, giving price-sensitive customers a lower barrier to a first purchase. Conversion rate is holding at parity. Buyer behavior deep-dive in progress.

Key Insight
The CAD Bundle Math email is outperforming the main discount email — $0.27 RPU vs. $0.02. This tells us pricing context and value framing work better for this audience than a straight percentage off. We're applying this learning directly.
What We Identified
The 20% welcome offer isn't resonating with this audience — they've been exposed to 30–40% discounts earlier in their journey. The data confirms it: 1.0% click rate and $0.02 RPU on the main offer email. The opportunity is clear, and the fix is already in motion.
Action This Week
Upgrading Email 1 to 30% off as the opening offer. This change is being implemented this week. We identified the gap between this audience's discount expectations and the current offer — the fix addresses it directly.
